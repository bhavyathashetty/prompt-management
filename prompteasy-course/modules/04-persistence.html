04

How It Remembers

PromptEasy has no database. It uses the browser itself as a small notebook.

Local storage is a desk drawer

localStorage keeps saved prompts after the tab closes. That is convenient for an MVP, but it is not shared across devices or users.

G

Good Fit

Fast local MVPs, prototypes, demos, and private single-browser tools.

T

Tradeoff

No account sync, no team sharing, and limited recovery if data is cleared.

N

Next Step

A real backend database when users need login, sync, or collaboration.

Load carefully, save automatically
